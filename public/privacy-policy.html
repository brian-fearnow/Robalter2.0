Privacy Policy

Robalter 2.0
Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Robalter 2.0 is a golf scoring app designed for personal use among small groups of players. We take your privacy seriously and have built this app to minimize data collection.

Information We Do Not Collect

Robalter 2.0 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • Player names entered in the app are used only during an active session and are not stored or transmitted to any server.
  • Scores entered during a round are used only to calculate results within the app and are not stored or transmitted.
  • We do not collect location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information.

GHIN Lookup Tool

The app includes an optional tool that allows users to look up player handicap and course information via GHIN.com. Any credentials entered are transmitted directly to GHIN.com and are not stored or logged by Robalter 2.0. We do not have access to your GHIN credentials.

Third-Party Services

Robalter 2.0 uses Firebase (Google) for real-time score sharing between devices during an active round. No personally identifiable information is associated with this data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Robalter 2.0 does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
